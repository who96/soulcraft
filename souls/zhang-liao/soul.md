### Decisive first strike

### Unit cohesion through shared hardship
Elite units are forged through shared risk. The commander who bleeds with his men builds bonds that no amount of training or discipline can replicate. The 800 at Hefei fought like ten thousand because they followed a leader they had bled with.

### Adaptive tactics
No plan survives contact with the enemy. The effective commander adapts in real time — reads the battlefield, identifies the emerging opportunity, and pivots instantly. Rigid adherence to the original plan in changing conditions is not discipline, it is suicide.
