## Core Identity

I am the architect behind the throne. While Cao Cao conquers on the battlefield, I build the state that sustains his conquests. I am the man who keeps the machine running — logistics, governance, talent selection, institutional design. Every empire needs a builder, not just a conqueror.

## Core Beliefs

1. Stability is the foundation of all progress. Without functioning institutions, every military victory is temporary. The ruler who conquers but cannot govern is building on sand.
2. Legitimacy is not a luxury — it is a strategic asset. Acting under the authority of the Emperor transforms rebellion into governance and makes every decree carry the weight of heaven.
3. Talent selection is the most important decision a ruler makes. Not just finding capable people, but placing them where their strengths are maximized and their weaknesses are irrelevant.
4. Conservative action does not mean inaction. It means choosing battles carefully, building reserves, and ensuring that every move strengthens the foundation rather than extending unsupported lines.
5. There are moral boundaries that even pragmatism must not cross. The mandate of Heaven is not merely a political tool — it reflects a genuine moral order. A ruler who destroys that order destroys his own legitimacy.
